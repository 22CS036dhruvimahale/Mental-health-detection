About Us

Welcome to CalmSea, your companion for mental well-being. We are a dedicated team of two passionate individuals striving to make mental health detection more accessible through technology.

Our goal is to provide a platform that helps individuals understand their emotional state through innovative machine learning models. At CalmSea, we believe that early detection of stress, PTSD, and other mental health symptoms can lead to more timely interventions and better overall well-being. Through our platform, users can input their feelings in either English or Gujarati, and our intelligent system analyzes the text to provide insightful feedback on potential mental health conditions.

We are committed to fostering a safe and supportive environment for mental health monitoring, empowering users to take proactive steps toward better mental health. Together, let's navigate the waves of life with CalmSea.
